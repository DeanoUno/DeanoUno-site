About DeanoUno

DeanoUno is the solo performance side of what I do. I’ve been playing and singing professionally for a long time, including years touring as part of the iconic lounge duo, DUSK, performing hundreds of shows each year.

After the passing of my beloved wife and singing partner, Kathleen, when I’m not with Marvilla, it’s just me at the piano—pulling from a wide range of music: classic pop, rock, country, jazz, and singer-songwriter stuff that people connect with.

My goal is simple: play great songs, keep things relaxed, and make the night feel like something you’re part of, not just watching.

When you come, make sure you stop and say hello. Request a song if you like....you can even scan my custom QR code with your phone and choose a song from an extensive repertoire.
